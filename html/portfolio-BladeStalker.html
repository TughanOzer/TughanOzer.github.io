Blade Stalker - Ludum Dare 54

2023

 

Blade Stalker takes you on a cyberpunk-esque journey through a neon-lit bar, where mastering the limited space bar is your path to finding your brother, all while remaining unseen by the futuristic city’s watchful eyes.

The game was made within 72h for the Ludum Dare 54 ("Jam"). The theme was "limited space".
