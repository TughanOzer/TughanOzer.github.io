ABEECALYPSE

2021

In Abeecalypse, bees have to flee from a poison cloud that threatens their colony. To find a new refuge they need to face innumerable dangers of nature. To be able to accomplish this mission, you need to fight enemies, collect ressources and forge new alliances.

Target Audience: Mobile, ShootEmUp, Rogue-Lite

Target Platform: Android

My contribution:
Role: Game Designer
- Game design (Core Loop, Gameplay, Items, Weapon variants, Enemies, Enemy behavior, Player Characters, Upgrades, etc.)
- Balancing
- Gameplay programming (Bullets, Weapon types(Shotgun, Bomb, Pistol, Laser), Upgrades, Health & Damage system, Boss)
- Programming of the shop and currency system. Randomized Items and Weapons
- Programming of the loot system
