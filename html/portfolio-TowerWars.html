Tower War

2020-2021

In this Chess-like Tabletop game three opposing players take control of an army of a variety of towers to take controll of the kingdom. The goal is simple: to capture and hold the central zone. But be carefull, the "fall" of your Lord will end YOUR kingdom, so protect your King at all cost!

3 Player, Chess-like, 20-40 minutes
University group project for a Tabletop game created with the usage of additive technologies.

Genre: Board Game, Strategy, Chess

My contribution:
Role: Gamedesigner
- Game idea
- Game design
- Testing, balancing and improvement of gameplay elements
- Creation of a digital prototype for playtesting inside "Tabletop Simulator" due to the pandemic
- 3d modelling of some of the towers (Chain-, Sun-, and Spear- tower and the stun-chip)
- 3d printing of the towers on my 3d printer
- Construction of the board
- Finding of a quick solution for high construction failure rates while in the manufacturing phase, with the help of additive technologies. Result: Reduction of failure rate to 0%
- Trailer animation
- Weekly presentation of our groups results
