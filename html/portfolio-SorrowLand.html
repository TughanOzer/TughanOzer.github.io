Sorrowland - Ludum Dare 53

2023

 

In this 2D point-and-click horror adventure you find yourself in the gloomy and empty place of Sorrowland, which is filled with creepy zombies who once shed their hearts out of sorrow and pain. But that is soon to come to an end! You play as the angel Mara, who was tasked with filling the zombies with their hearts. However, that is easier said than done, as Mara must first confront the history of the zombies and restore their memories with the help of memory pieces.

Created as part of the Ludum Dare 53 jam in under 72 hours
